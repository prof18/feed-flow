Features

Read, sync, organize.

Everything you need to follow the open web — nothing you don't.

Read your way

Open articles in your default browser or pick a dedicated one for reading sessions. Switch to Reader Mode for a distraction-free view with adjustable text size. Prefetch full content for offline reading — perfect for commutes or flights.

Sync that fits your setup

Keep everything local, or sync across devices your way. iCloud for Apple devices. Dropbox or Google Drive for cross-platform cloud sync. Self-hosted services: FreshRSS, Miniflux, Feedbin, BazQux. No lock-in, ever.

Stay organized

Import and export with OPML — migrate from any other reader in minutes. Organize feeds into categories, bookmark articles to read later, block noisy keywords, and search across everything.
