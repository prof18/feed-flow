Just a list.

All you need is a de-cluttered list. Every article opens in the browser of your choice or in a clutter-free reader mode. No more half-available content, multiple clicks to read something. Just focus on reading (or scrolling)!

Quickly import your existing RSS feeds collection

Sync your feeds across all your devices with Dropbox, iCloud (only iOS and macOS) and FreshRSS.

Focus on reading.

Enjoy full control - open articles in your favourite privacy-focused browser and keep your reading experience separated from your main browser. Or use the integrated clutter-free reader mode.

  • Import your existing collection with one click.

  • Use your favourite browser or the reader mode

  • Built with Compose, SwiftUI, Kotlin Multiplatform

Frequently asked questions

Most websites with an RSS feed don't share the article's content because they want you to go on their website. For this reason, FeedFlow always opens the original website, but you can choose the browser you want. For example, you can open an article on DuckDuckGo or Firefox Focus with all the trackers disabled and then kill all the navigation data. This way, the reading experience is separated from your main browser instance. FeedFlow also offers a reader mode that downloads the full content of the article and renders it in a clutter-free view.
In the app, you can import the OPML file for your RSS collection. You can easily generate that from your current RSS reader.
