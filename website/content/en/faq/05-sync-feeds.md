---
title: "Can I sync my feeds across devices?"
weight: 5
---

Yes! FeedFlow supports multiple sync options:

- **Dropbox Sync**: Sync your feeds using your Dropbox account
- **FreshRSS**: Connect to your self-hosted FreshRSS instance
- **iCloud**: Sync across Apple devices (iOS and macOS)

You can configure sync in the app settings under "Feed Synchronization".
